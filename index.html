How to Play

Getting started in Nationwide is simple, but mastering it takes strategy and cunning

1

Choose Your Nation

Select your capital city location, nation name, and territorial color. Your starting position will determine your resources and strategic options.

2

Submit Orders

Each turn, submit orders covering everything from infrastructure development to military movements and diplomatic negotiations.

3

Interact & Strategize

Communicate with other players through TPT comments or Discord. Form alliances, negotiate trades, or plan your next conquest.

4

Evolve & Expand

Develop your civilization from primitive beginnings to spacefaring empire. Research technology, colonize worlds, dominate the galaxy.

How to Play

Getting started in Nationwide is simple, but mastering it takes strategy and cunning

1

Choose Your Nation

Select your capital city location, nation name, and territorial color. Your starting position will determine your resources and strategic options.

2

Submit Orders

Each "turn", submit orders covering everything from infrastructure development to military movements and diplomatic negotiations, or all at once.

3

Interact & Strategize

Communicate with other players through TPT comments or Discord. Form alliances, negotiate trades, or plan your next war. Meta gaming is allowed.

4

Evolve & Expand

Develop your civilization from its beginnings to a large spacefaring empire. Research technology, colonize worlds, and try to dominate the galaxy.

Game Systems

All modeled uniquely, these are the interconnected systems that determine your nation's success

Geography & Resources

Your location determines access to materials, trade routes, and strategic advantages.

Economy

Generate wealth through industry, trade, and resource exploitation.

Politics

Maintain internal stability while making allies or enemies.

Military

Develop defense capabilities and infrastructure for conquest or defense

Infrastructure

Build roads, railways, ports, and airports to connect your territory.

Technology

Research and development following loosely historical progression paths, dependent on the host.

Species Design

Create and customize unique alien species with distinct characteristics, dependent on the host.

Space Operations

Launch satellites, colonize planets, develop space industry, and master FTL travel.

Magic

Host determined magical systems ensure everything is balanced, as all things should be.
